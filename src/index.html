Welcome to Effective Studying!

This website is a coding project done by Jungsu Kim.

This website allows the user to study more effectively! What I mean by that is, it is scientically proven that long study session is a bad method of studying! What is recommended is that you study in intervals of 20, 30, 40, and etc with breaks in-between those intervals. That is why I have created this website. This website does basically nothing for 20, 30, 40 and etc minutes, however, after it launches a little mini game for your break! These mini games are snake, pacman, tetris, pong and flappy bird. You can play these games for 5 or 10 minutes.
